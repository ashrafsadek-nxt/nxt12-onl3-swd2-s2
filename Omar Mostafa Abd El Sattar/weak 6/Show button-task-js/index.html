Mercedes Benz AMG C63S

is a high-performance variant of the C-Class, powered by a handcrafted 4.0L twin-turbo V8 producing around 503 hp and 700 Nm of torque. The car blends luxury and sportiness with premium materials, advanced tech, and aggressive AMG styling handling and aerodynamics

Mercedes-AMG GT

is a front mid-engine, rear-wheel-drive sports car powered by a handcrafted 4.0L twin-turbo V8, producing up to 720 hp in top variants. It delivers blistering performance, with 0–100 km/h times starting from 3.1 seconds, paired with precise AMG handling and aerodynamics.

BMW M3

is a high-performance sports sedan powered by a 3.0L twin-turbo inline-6 engine, producing up to 503 hp in the Competition variant. It accelerates from 0–100 km/h in as little as 3.4 seconds, featuring adaptive M suspension and precision steering with precise handling.

BMW M4

is a high-performance coupe (and convertible) powered by a 3.0L twin-turbo inline-6, producing up to 503 hp in the Competition version. It accelerates from 0–100 km/h in as little as 3.5 seconds, with precise handling and adaptive M suspension.
